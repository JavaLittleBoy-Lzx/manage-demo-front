# Product Overview

## 雪人停车管理系统 (Snowman Parking Management System)

Enterprise-grade parking management SaaS platform designed for comprehensive parking facility operations.

### Core Purpose
- Parking facility management and operations
- Multi-community support with role-based access control
- Complete business workflow from personnel to vehicle management
- Equipment maintenance and data analytics

### Key Business Modules
- **System Management**: User roles, permissions, staff management
- **Community Management**: Property info, residents, access control
- **Daily Operations**: Approvals, device procurement, announcements  
- **Vehicle Management**: Visitor vehicles, reservations, blacklists, monthly passes
- **Asset Management**: Equipment allocation, maintenance, disposal
- **Analytics**: Reporting and data queries

### Target Users
- Parking facility operators
- Property management companies
- Community administrators
- Security personnel and patrol officers

### Business Model
Multi-tenant SaaS platform supporting multiple communities with hierarchical permission management.
